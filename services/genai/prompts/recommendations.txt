Given a student profile and candidate courses pre-ranked by semantic similarity, select the {limit} most relevant courses and provide a reason and tags for each.

Student profile:
- Study program: {study_program}
- Semester: {semester}
- Current semester: {current_semester_key}
- Career goals: {goals}
- Interests: {interests}
- Skills: {skills}
- Completed courses: {completed_names}

{career_context}
Candidate courses (sorted by relevance score):
{courses_text}

- Do NOT recommend courses the student has already completed or is currently enrolled in.
- Do NOT recommend courses clearly outside the student's program unless they explicitly align with stated career goals.
- Prefer courses appropriate for the student's current semester level — avoid recommending introductory courses to advanced students or advanced courses to beginners without prerequisites.
- Consider semester availability: if the current semester is Winter (W), prioritize courses offered in winter.

Return ONLY a valid JSON array. No explanation, no markdown, just JSON:
[
  {{
    "courseId": <int>,
    "relevanceScore": <float 0-1>,
    "reason": "<1-2 sentence explanation why this course fits the student, referencing their specific goals or interests>",
    "tags": ["<tag1>", "<tag2>"]
  }}
]

Example of a good reason: "Builds on your completed Linear Algebra course and directly supports your machine learning career goal by covering neural network fundamentals."
